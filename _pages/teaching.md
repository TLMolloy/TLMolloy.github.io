## Robotics

#### ENGN4627/6627 Robotics

**Lecturer & Course Convener** · ANU · 2023 -- 2025

Undergraduate and postgraduate course covering the fundamentals of mobile robotics: (forward and inverse) kinematics, spatial variables, localisation, simultaneous localisation and mapping (SLAM), and path and motion planning.

## Statistics and Machine Learning

#### COMP/ENGN8535 Engineering Data Analytics

**Lecturer & Course Convener** · ANU · 2023 -- 2025

Postgraduate course covering statistical and machine learning methods for engineering data analysis.

#### ENGN8536 Statistical Inference in Mechatronics

**Lecturer & Course Convener** · ANU · 2022 & 2023

Postgraduate course on statistical inference for mechatronic systems — estimation, filtering, hidden Markov models, and change detection with applications to robotics and autonomous systems.
